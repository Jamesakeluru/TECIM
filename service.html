THURSDAY BIBLE STUDIES

Join us for our Thursday Bible Study sessions, where we gather together to delve deeper into the word of God. This is a time of reflection, learning, and fellowship as we grow in our faith and understanding of God’s will. Bible study is a powerful opportunity to come closer to the Lord and find strength in His word, especially in times of uncertainty.

‘Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path.’ (Psalm 119:105) Through Bible study, we illuminate the path of our lives and align ourselves with God’s guidance. As we study together, we encourage one another to live according to His truth and righteousness.

‘The Lord is near to all who call on Him, to all who call on Him in truth.’ (Psalm 145:18) In our study, we invite you to call on God’s presence, to receive wisdom and peace as we explore His word and how it applies to our daily lives.

We look forward to growing together in the faith and being transformed by the power of the Scriptures.

SUNDAY SERVICE

Join us for our Sunday Service, a day to celebrate the love and grace of God. Our Sunday service is a time of worship, prayer, and teaching, where we lift our voices in praise and seek God’s direction for the week ahead. Together, we acknowledge the sacrifice of Jesus Christ and embrace the hope that He offers us through His resurrection.

‘This is the day the Lord has made; let us rejoice and be glad in it.’ (Psalm 118:24) Every Sunday is an opportunity to celebrate the goodness of God and reflect on His faithfulness. As we come together in worship, we remember His sovereignty over our lives and His unchanging love.

‘For where two or three are gathered in my name, there am I with them.’ (Matthew 18:20) We believe that wherever we gather, whether virtually or physically, Christ’s presence is with us, uniting us as one body of believers. Let us lift our hearts in worship and experience the fullness of His love as we gather together.
